Typography

Typography is an interesting aspect of design. It can be applied in most of the other principles of design. Contributing to the look and feel of a web page,typography can also be an important part of how the webpage is read. Font size, line spacing, text hierarchy are all factors in how a user may navigate the website. Squarespace does a unique job using typography. I think it is used in a more minimalist way. They dont use a lot of text, but with choice of words, type, size, and font choice, they make their point in a simple and elegant way.
