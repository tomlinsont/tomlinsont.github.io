Alignment

Looking at killersites.com you can see quite easily the principle of alignment. This stands out immediately in the two columns of the main portion of the website. Starting on the left side of the page, you can see how everything lines up right down the entire page. From the logo at the top, to the page title in the header, down through the paragraphs and images in the first column. The same organization is found in the second column. Alignment contributes to the look and feel of the web page. Through applying elements in an organized layout like a grid pattern you can create unity in the appearance of the site.
