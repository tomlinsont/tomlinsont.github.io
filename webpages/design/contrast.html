Contrast

Contrast is the use of not only opposites, but differant elements such as size, shapes, colors to help direct the users eye to see what you want the user to see. The star wars site uses a lot of differant elements of contrast. From the use of various fonts, to colors. Colors are used not just in opposites, but even more so in categories of feel, like warm or cold colors. There are a lot of more neutral colors like black, browns, and white.
