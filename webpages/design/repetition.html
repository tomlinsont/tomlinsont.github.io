Repetition

In repetition we are using elements over and over again to get a desired effect. On this web site we see some repetition in the layout, using somewhat of a block or grid layout. There is also repetition in the the fonts used on the site. Mostly I would say that the repetion is found primarily in the use of cool colors. This would definately go hand in hand with the consept of the cooler they are trying to sell.
